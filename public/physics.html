#
Magnetic Force

In my opinion today's magnetic force equation that uses the right hand law is wrong. The thing that triggered me the most:
Both particles are positivly charged. The green particle goes right, and the yellow particle goes up. As you can see the yellow particle pushes green down, and the green particle doesnt creates any force at all on yellow.



So I was trying to find new equation that will make a lot more sense. If you wanna see how i found a new equation, check [my equation discovery](discovering_equation.html) (first half is boring). If you wanna see the cool features of the new equation, check the [Different Situations](different_situations.html) (very interesting).
